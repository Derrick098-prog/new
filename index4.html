McKEON ASSEMBLY

MCkeon Assembly is the first english assembly established in the church It has been mainly formed to help people have more interest in partaking in English services and also people who have difficulty in speaking ewe and twi since they are the languages mostly spoken at the other branches. They normally fellowship early morning on sundays from 6:30am to 9:00am to enable members to close early to go attend to other things if need. It is a very youthful assembly since english interests the youth. There are over 150 memebers that fellowship at McKeown a assembly. It being presided over by Elder Richard Vordagba. The church was was named as McKeown in honor of the founder of the church. Pastor James McKeown of blessed memory.
