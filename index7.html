HOUSING ASSEMBLY

Housing assembly is a branch under fiave district.It is the second oldest and largest assembly under the fiave district.It has a population of about two hundred members. It is located at Ho-Housing. The presiding elder of housing is Elder Zartey. He took over from Elder Stephen Novihoho who has been transfered to central to serve there. Elder Philip Mamatah who is also the general secrecaty of the fiave distrit fellowship at Housing where he was once a presiding eder. Elder Dagadu is also another elder that supports the presiding elder in the day-to-day affairs the branch. Church service commence on Sundays from 8:30am to 11:30am. Other church activities that goes on during week days are normally announced to the congregation on sundays during service.
