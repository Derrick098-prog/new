GRACE ASSEMBLY


Grace assembly is the most recently formed assembly in the fiave district. It is the assembly where French and English are being spoken for the benefit of the members who are from the Francophone countries and some local members who are interested in learning French. Its has a population of about hundred and it is being presided over by Elder Adolf Lemtha popularly known as Soldier. He is being assisted by elder wisdom Adegah. The youngest Elder in the Fiave district.
