FIAVE CENTRAL

Fiave cetral is the the biggest branch in the fiave district. It is also the mother church of the district where all the other branches are being nurtured from and it has the largest population with about three- hundred and fifty members in the district. It is being presided over by Elder Anthony Adegah and he is being assisted by Elder Stephen Novihoho who is the finacial secretary of the district, Elder Emmanuel Odame whom is also the leader/father of all the youths in the district,Elder Prosper Tenu who also retired just recently and was once a presiding elder for fiave central branch and Elder Samuel Azar and Some other elders. All these elders provides supporting hands to thee presiding and also the pastor for things to run smoothly in the church. The fiave central chapel is located on the Bob Coffie's street. One of the current Executive council members of the church of pentecost was once the presiding Pastor of Fiave district few years back before he was transfered to south korea for international missions. I myself and my family fellowship at fiave central. Service begins on Sundays from 9:00 am and closes at 11:30 am. Every other church activities that goes on within the week are normally announced on Sundays
