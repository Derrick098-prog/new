SYCHAR ASSEMBLY

Sychar assembly is a branch under fiave district.It is the third largest assembly under the fiave district.It has a population of about two hundred members. It is located at Ho-Some down. The presiding elder of Sychar assembly is Elder Eugene Tordzro who was the past general secrertary of the fiave district and he is being supported by Elder Emmanuel Darko and some other elders of the church. They normally fellowship on every Sundays from 8:30 am to 11:30 am. Elder Emmanuel Darko is one of the logest and oldest serving elders of the church.
